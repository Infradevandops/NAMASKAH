Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated: January 18, 2025

Namaskah SMS ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our SMS verification service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted), name (optional)
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Paystack (we don't store card details)
  • API Keys: Generated keys for programmatic access
  • Support Communications: Messages sent through our support system

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Verification history, services used, timestamps
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Cookies: Session cookies, authentication tokens, preferences
  • Analytics: Page views, click events, user flows (anonymized)

1.3 Third-Party Information

  • Google OAuth: Email, profile information (if you sign in with Google)
  • Payment Provider: Transaction status, payment method type

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Delivery

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process SMS verification requests
  • Handle payments and billing
  • Send service notifications (verification status, low balance alerts)
  • Provide customer support

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Monitor service performance and uptime
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Conduct security audits

2.3 Communication

  • Send transactional emails (receipts, confirmations)
  • Notify you of service updates or changes
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent, opt-out available)

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 We Share Information With:

  • Service Providers: Paystack (payments), TextVerified (SMS delivery), Google (authentication)
  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics (anonymized data)
  • Error Tracking: Sentry (for debugging, no personal data)

3.2 We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your phone numbers for marketing
  • Store your payment card details

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: HTTPS/TLS for all data transmission
  • Password Security: Bcrypt hashing with salt
  • Access Control: Role-based permissions, admin-only areas
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against brute force attacks
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and penetration testing

4.2 Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while account is active
  • Verification History: 90 days for active users, 30 days after account deletion
  • Transaction Records: 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Logs: 30 days for security and debugging

5. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

5.1 You Have the Right To:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Export your data in machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions at any time

5.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

Email us at privacy@namaskah.app with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies and Tracking

6.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for authentication and security
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences (theme, language)
  • Analytics Cookies: Track usage patterns (Google Analytics)

6.2 Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that some features may not work without cookies.

7. Third-Party Services

7.1 Payment Processing (Paystack)

Payment data is processed by Paystack. We do not store your card details. Review Paystack's privacy policy at paystack.com/privacy

7.2 SMS Delivery (TextVerified)

Phone numbers are provided by TextVerified. Temporary numbers are not linked to your identity. Review TextVerified's privacy policy at their website.

7.3 Google OAuth

If you sign in with Google, we receive your email and profile information. Review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or prominent notice on our website. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

  • Email: privacy@namaskah.app
  • Support: support@namaskah.app
  • Website: namaskah.app/contact

12. Data Protection Officer

For GDPR-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@namaskah.app
