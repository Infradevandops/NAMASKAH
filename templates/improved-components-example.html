Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a receipt for my payments?

+

Yes! All payments are automatically tracked in your account. You can:

  • View transaction history in your dashboard
  • Download receipts as PDF
  • Request detailed invoices via email

For business accounts, we provide monthly consolidated invoices.

How do I contact support?

+

We offer multiple support channels:

  • Email: support@namaskah.app (24-48 hour response)
  • In-app support tickets (fastest response)
  • Live chat during business hours

Premium users get priority support with guaranteed 12-hour response time.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We support multiple payment options:

  • Paystack (NGN) - Cards, Bank Transfer, USSD
  • Bitcoin (BTC)
  • Ethereum (ETH)
  • Solana (SOL)
  • USDT (Tether)

All payments are processed securely with 256-bit SSL encryption.
