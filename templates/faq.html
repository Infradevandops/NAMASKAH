What are webhooks and how do I use them? +

Webhooks allow you to receive SMS notifications automatically at your server URL. Available on Developer Plan and Enterprise Plan.

How it works:

  1. Add your webhook URL in the dashboard (must be HTTPS)
  2. When an SMS arrives, we POST to your URL with the message
  3. Your server receives instant notifications
