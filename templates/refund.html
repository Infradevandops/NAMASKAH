Refund Policy

Fair and transparent refund terms

Last Updated: January 18, 2025
Our Commitment: We offer automatic refunds for failed verifications and manual refunds for cancellations. Your satisfaction is our priority.

1. Automatic Refunds

You automatically receive a full refund if:

1.1 SMS Not Received

  • No SMS arrives within the service timeout period (varies by service)
  • Typical timeout: 10-30 minutes depending on service
  • Refund is instant and credited to your wallet
  • No action required from you

1.2 Technical Failures

  • Phone number is invalid or blocked
  • Service provider error
  • System malfunction on our end
  • API timeout or connection issues

1.3 Refund Timeline

Instant: Automatic refunds are credited to your wallet immediately when failure is detected.

2. Manual Refunds (Cancellations)

2.1 How to Cancel

You can cancel a verification and receive a refund if:

  • SMS has not been received yet
  • Verification status is "pending"
  • You cancel before timeout period expires

2.2 Cancellation Process

  1. Go to your verification history
  2. Click "Cancel" on the pending verification
  3. Confirm cancellation
  4. Refund is instant (credited to wallet)

2.3 Refund Amount

100% refund if canceled before SMS is received.

3. Non-Refundable Scenarios

Important: The following situations are NOT eligible for refunds:

3.1 Completed Verifications

  • SMS was successfully received
  • Verification status is "completed"
  • You used the verification code

3.2 User Error

  • Wrong service selected (your mistake)
  • Incorrect service name entered
  • Changed your mind after SMS received

3.3 Third-Party Issues

  • Service (WhatsApp, Telegram, etc.) blocks the number
  • Service is down or unavailable
  • Service changed their verification system
  • Geographic restrictions by the service

3.4 Wallet Funding

  • Payment processor fees (Paystack charges)
  • Currency conversion fees
  • Wallet deposits cannot be withdrawn as cash
  • Credits can only be used for services

4. Number Rental Refunds

4.1 Early Release

If you release a rented number before expiration:

  • Refund: 50% of unused time
  • Minimum: 1 hour must be used
  • Calculation: (Unused hours / Total hours) × 50% × Cost

4.2 Example

Scenario: You rent a number for 7 days (N5) but release after 2 days.
Used: 2 days (48 hours)
Unused: 5 days (120 hours)
Refund: (120/168) × 50% × N5 = N1.79

4.3 No Refund for Rentals

  • Expired rentals (full duration used)
  • Rentals with less than 1 hour remaining
  • Rentals that received SMS (service was used)

5. Subscription Refunds

5.1 Developer & Enterprise Tiers

Pricing tiers are based on total funded amount, not subscriptions. No refunds for tier upgrades.

5.2 Unused Credits

  • Credits remain in your wallet indefinitely
  • No expiration date
  • Can be used anytime
  • Cannot be withdrawn as cash

6. Refund Methods

6.1 Wallet Credit (Default)

All refunds are credited to your Namaskah wallet as credits (N). You can use these credits for future verifications.

6.2 Cash Refunds

Not available. Due to payment processor fees and instant service delivery, we do not offer cash refunds. All refunds are wallet credits only.

6.3 Exceptions

Cash refunds may be considered in exceptional circumstances:

  • Service permanently discontinued
  • Account banned due to our error
  • Legal requirement

Contact support@namaskah.app for exceptional cases.

7. Dispute Resolution

7.1 Refund Disputes

If you believe you're entitled to a refund that was denied:

  1. Email support@namaskah.app with details
  2. Include verification ID and screenshots
  3. We will review within 24-48 hours
  4. Decision is final

7.2 Chargeback Policy

Important: Initiating a chargeback without contacting us first may result in:
  • Immediate account suspension
  • Forfeiture of all credits
  • Ban from future use

Always contact support first. We're here to help resolve issues fairly.

8. Fraud Prevention

8.1 Abuse of Refunds

Excessive refund requests or patterns of abuse may result in:

  • Account review
  • Refund restrictions
  • Account suspension
  • Permanent ban

8.2 What Constitutes Abuse

  • Requesting refunds for completed verifications
  • Canceling after receiving SMS
  • Creating multiple accounts for refunds
  • False claims of non-delivery

9. Special Circumstances

9.1 Service Outages

If our service is down for extended periods (4+ hours), we may offer:

  • Bonus credits as compensation
  • Extended rental periods
  • Refunds for affected verifications

9.2 Account Closure

If you close your account:

  • Remaining credits are forfeited
  • No cash refund for unused balance
  • Pending verifications are canceled with refund
  • Active rentals are released with 50% refund

10. Contact Us

For refund-related questions or disputes:

  • Email: support@namaskah.app
  • Subject Line: "Refund Request - [Verification ID]"
  • Response Time: Within 24-48 hours

11. Policy Changes

We may update this refund policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance.
